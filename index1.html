Indian

Indian cuisine is diverse, featuring rich gravies, spicy flavors, and rice-based dishes. Common ingredients include a variety of spices, legumes, and fresh herbs, with popular dishes like biryani and dosa.

Chinese

Chinese cuisine is diverse, featuring regional styles like Cantonese, Sichuan, and Shandong. Common ingredients include rice, noodles, soy sauce, and a variety of vegetables and meats.

Russian

Russian cuisine is diverse, featuring hearty soups, dumplings, and pastries. Common ingredients include potatoes, cabbage, and various meats.
